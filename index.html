The distribution of water on Earth is highly uneven.This uneven distribution poses challenges for accessing clean freshwater resources, with many regions facing water scarcity and competition for limited freshwater supplies, making sustainable water management a global concern. Global water cycle

The global water cycle, also known as the hydrological cycle, is the continuous movement of water on, above, and below the Earth's surface. It describes the circulation and exchange of water between the atmosphere, land, oceans, and living organisms. Here's a brief overview of the global water cycle.
